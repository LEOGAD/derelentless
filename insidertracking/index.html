What is insider wallet tracking?

Not financial advice. Not a signal group you blindly copy. A method I use myself.

It isn't luck. It's pattern recognition — finding those wallets, studying how they enter, how they exit, and how their early wallet groups move together. Once you can read that pattern, you stop reacting to pumps and start anticipating them.
